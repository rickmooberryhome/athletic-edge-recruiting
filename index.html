Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 360° Review work?
You fill out our form with your academic info, stats, highlight video link, and college preferences. Within 24 hours, we'll send you a personalized recruiting roadmap tailored to your profile.

That depends on your plan. Foundation and Elite give you the tools to reach out yourself. Signature includes full coach outreach where we handle the communication for you.
What if I don't have a highlight video yet?
No problem. We'll give you a simple guide on creating a 30-60 second highlight reel that coaches actually want to see. Plus, Elite and Signature plans include our team reviewing and improving your tape.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 7 days notice for the next billing cycle.
