About Athletic Edge

We're athletes, coaches, and parents who know the recruiting game.

Our Story

Athletic Edge was born out of frustration. We watched good athletes — great athletes — slip through the cracks because they didn't have the time, knowledge, or connections to navigate the college recruiting process.

We're not a recruiting service that promises the impossible. We're not agents. We're not scouts. We're your partner in the process — doing the research, the outreach, and the follow-up so you can focus on playing.

Whether you're a freshman just starting to think about recruiting or a senior looking to maximize your options, we're here to help you find the right fit — not just any offer.

Why We Do This

We've Been There

Our team includes former college athletes, high school coaches, and parents who've navigated the recruiting process firsthand.

No Gimmicks

We don't promise scholarships. We don't guarantee offers. We promise hard work, honest feedback, and a team in your corner.

Local Roots

We started in Iowa, and we know the recruiting landscape for Midwest athletes. But we work with athletes nationwide.
